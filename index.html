Learning from interventions with hierarchical policies for safe learning

We propose a novel algorithm which combines Learning from Interventions with Hierarchical Imitation Learning. We also propose an interpolation trick called, Backtracking, that allows us to use state-action pairs before and after the intervention. Besides, a Triplet-Network based architecture which is capable of training the hierarchical policies.

2019
July - Sept.

Video Prediction

In this period, We implemented and evluated comparative evaluation among state-of-the-art Sequence Prediction and Generativemodels including ConvLSTM-2D/3D, Pred-Net/RNN++, VAE-GAN variations, and Pixel-RNN etc.

Besides, We bring the idea of deformable convolution into this project, and developed a motion matrix that contains normal image transformation function with learn parameter for disentanglemotion and content in moving video sequence. At last, we designed autoregressive 3d-Wavenet model and achieved competitive result with higher parallel performance.

Navigation by Imitation in a Pedestrian-Rich Environment

In this work, we introduced an Interactive learn-from-intervention algorithm with hierarchical framework for safer and faster learningthe control policy from real-world human behavior.

Besides, we built physical structure of an outdoor autonomous car with Nvidia TX2 and Arduino, and designed control and recording software. The proposed approach isApplied to a robotic vehicle in real-world campus sidewalk settingand the result indicates our method is data efficient approach for generalizing agood decision while ensuring safety

Incremental learning with VAE

we analyzed the current state of the art method for class incremental learning, iCaRL, in detail, and showed that the primary reason iCaRL works well is not because of the herding algorithm. We also proposed a simple yet effective threshold moving method to fix the bias introduced by the distillation loss, and verified the effectiveness of this method. Finally, we use VAE to dis- cover the underlying feature distribution and use it to select representative samples, which help the distillation part obtain more meaningful samples form the previous trained dataset.

Sept. - Dec.

Autonomous General Object Tracking with Unmanned Aerial Vehicles (UAVs)

In this work, we first proposaled a new framework to track general object with UAV. By combining a regression model for estimating object velocity using camera and drone dynamic measurement and a Siamese network based structure that is used to taking estimated distance of moving object as conditional input,The final tracking system is able to track assigned general moving objects offline in real-world with 30FPS.
